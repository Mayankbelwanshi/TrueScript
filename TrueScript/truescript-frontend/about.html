About TrueScript

TrueScript is a powerful plagiarism detection tool designed specifically for code. Whether you're a student, teacher, or developer, TrueScript helps ensure the originality of your code and protect intellectual property. Our advanced algorithm checks for similarities across a wide range of programming languages.

  • Real-time plagiarism detection
  • Comprehensive plagiarism reports with detailed breakdowns
  • Secure and private platform
  • Fast and easy to use
